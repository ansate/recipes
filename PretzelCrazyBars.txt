Pretzel Crazy Bars

16 bars

* Prep Time  : 15 mins
* Cook Time  : 25 mins
* Total Time : 40 mins

Ingredients:

* 6 oz of pretzels, crushed
* 1 stick (8 Tblsp) butter, melted
* 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk
* 1 1/3 c of shredded coconut
* 2 c chocolate chips

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
2. Line 9x13 pan with parchment
3. Mix the crushed pretzels and butter, and press into the bottom of a 9x13 pan to form a crumb crust. 
4. Pour about 1/4 of the condensed milk on top
5. Spread coconut across the top
6. Pour about 1/4 of the condensed milk on top
7. Top with chocolate chips
8. Cover with remaining condensed milk
9. Bake for 25 minutes, until edges start to brown

It will be easier to cut them into bars if you cool them in the fridge or freezer first
